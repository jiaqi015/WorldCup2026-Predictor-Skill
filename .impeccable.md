## Design Context

### Users
World Cup viewers use the predictor to simulate groups, advance through the
knockout bracket, and compare a complete tournament story at a glance.

### Brand Personality
Compact, calm, and confident. The interface should feel like a polished
tournament tool rather than a game dashboard full of decorative effects.

### Aesthetic Direction
Information-dense editorial utility with restrained neutral surfaces and rare
gold accents for the champion path. Support both light and dark themes.

### Design Principles
- Preserve bracket density and never enlarge cards for secondary information.
- Prefer small typographic or inline state cues over badges and extra panels.
- Keep hierarchy quiet; reserve gold for the champion path and final outcomes.
- Make state differences readable without relying on color alone.
- Verify visible changes in the local browser at desktop and narrow widths.
